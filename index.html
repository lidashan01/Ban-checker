About Reddit Shadowban Checker

What is a Reddit Shadowban?

A shadowban (or stealth ban) on Reddit is a special type of ban where a user's account appears normal to them, but all their posts, comments, and their profile are invisible to the rest of the community. This is Reddit's way of dealing with spammers and rule-breakers without tipping them off. Our Reddit Shadowban Checker helps you see what others see.

Why Do Reddit Accounts Get Shadowbanned?

Accounts are typically shadowbanned for violating Reddit's content policy, often related to spamming, vote manipulation, or posting prohibited content. To avoid this, always engage authentically, avoid mass-messaging or link-dropping, and read the rules of each subreddit you participate in. The Reddit Shadowban Checker is a good first step to diagnose if your marketing or engagement efforts have been flagged.

How to Appeal a Shadowban

If you believe your account was shadowbanned by mistake, you have one primary way to appeal. Visit the official Reddit Appeals Page. Be polite, concise, and honest in your appeal. Explain why you think the shadowban was an error. Do not create new accounts to evade the ban, as this is against Reddit's rules and can lead to a permanent site-wide ban.
