Understanding the Reddit Shadowban

What is a Reddit Shadowban?

A shadowban (also known as a stealth ban) on Reddit means that your account appears normal to you, but your posts, comments, and profile are invisible to everyone else. It's a way for admins to handle spammers and rule-breakers without alerting them that action has been taken. You can still vote and post, but your contributions won't be seen by the community.

How Does This Shadowban Checker Work?

Our tool checks for shadowbans by attempting to access a user's profile data via Reddit's public API. If a profile exists and is accessible, the account is likely normal. If the profile data cannot be retrieved, it often indicates the user is either shadowbanned or the account does not exist. This ban checker provides a quick and reliable way to diagnose an account's status.

What To Do If You Are Shadowbanned?

If you believe your account has been shadowbanned by mistake, the only way to resolve it is to contact the Reddit admins. You can send an appeal to them by visiting www.reddit.com/appeal. Be polite and explain why you believe the ban was an error. Do not create a new account to evade the ban, as this is against Reddit's rules.
