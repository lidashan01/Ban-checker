About the YouTube Ban Checker

Our tool provides a quick and reliable way to check the status of any YouTube channel. By using the official YouTube Data API, we can determine if a channel is active, has been terminated by YouTube for policy violations, or was deleted by its owner.

When you enter a channel's identifier (like its handle, custom URL, or ID), our tool queries the YouTube API. A successful response means the channel is active. If the API reports that the channel cannot be found, it is flagged as "Banned or Not Found."
